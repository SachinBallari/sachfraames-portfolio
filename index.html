02 / ABOUT

Hi, I'm Sachin Ballari.

I'm a filmmaker and content creator based in Karnataka. What started as a passion for capturing moments has grown into creating visual stories for brands, restaurants, creators, and businesses.

I enjoy planning shots, finding creative angles, and turning simple ideas into engaging content. From commercial reels and brand films to event coverage and social media content, my focus is always on storytelling. I believe every frame should have a purpose, and every story should leave a lasting impression.
